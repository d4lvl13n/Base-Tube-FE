# Creator's Reward System

Our Creator's Reward System is thoughtfully tailored to celebrate and incentivize our content creators. Understanding their crucial role in our ecosystem, this system is designed to reward creators for their quality, consistency, and engagement with the community. Our tiered structure aims to cultivate a flourishing environment where every creator's contribution is valued and recognized.

#### **For Creators**

1. **Innovator Tier** _(Default)_
   * Upload up to 3 videos per week.
   * Access to basic analytics.
2. **Visionary Tier**
   * Hold a certain amount of $TUBE tokens.
   * Enhanced earnings per view.
   * Upload up to 5 videos per week.
   * Access to enhanced analytics.
3. **Luminary Tier**
   * Own a membership NFT.
   * Highest earnings per view.
   * Unlimited uploads.
   * Featured content placement and advanced analytics.
4. **Guardian Tier**
   * Hold tokens and a membership NFT.
   * Priority featuring and community leader badge.
