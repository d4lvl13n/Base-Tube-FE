#### **A Hypothetical Scenario**

_Imagine Maria, an aspiring musician with a small but dedicated following. On Base.Tube, she releases a new song exclusively through an NFT Content Pass. Her fans purchase the pass to access not only the song but also exclusive behind-the-scenes videos and live Q\&A sessions._
