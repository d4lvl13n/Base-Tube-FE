# Reward-based Tier System for Viewers

Our Viewer Reward System is designed to actively engage and reward our audience. Utilizing a tiered structure, this system escalates the benefits for viewers based on their level of interaction and investment in the platform, from casual viewing to dedicated support.

### **Tiered Reward System**

### **For Viewers**

1. **Explorer Tier** _(Default)_
   * Access to standard features and content.
   * Earn rewards for up to 2 hours of viewing per day.
2. **Enthusiast Tier**
   * Hold a certain amount of $TUBE tokens.
   * Extended reward-earning potential.
3. **Luminary Tier**
   * Purchase an exclusive NFT Content Pass.
   * Unlimited earning potential with no daily cap.
   * Access to premium content and features.
4. **Guardian Tier**
   * Hold $TUBE tokens and an exclusive NFT.
   * All previous benefits plus an ad-free experience.



**Conclusion**: This tiered system not only serves to reward our viewers but also plays a crucial role in sustaining a vibrant and engaged community. It encourages viewers to delve deeper into the platform, transitioning from casual viewers to committed community members, and contributes to a thriving, dynamic ecosystem.
