# Tiers Details



| **Tier**                                                                                                | **Viewer Access Criteria**                                                                                                                                                   | **Creator Access Criteria**                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Innovator Tier** (Viewers) / **Free Tier** (Creators)                                                 | <p>- Default tier upon signing up. </p><p>- Access to standard platform features and content. </p><p>- Limited to earning rewards for 2 hours of video watching per day.</p> | <p>- Default tier upon signing up as a creator. </p><p>- Ability to upload up to 3 videos per week. </p><p>- Access to basic analytics.</p>                                                                                             |
| <p><br><strong>Enthusiast Tier</strong> (Viewers)/</p><p><strong>Visionary Tier</strong> (Creators)</p> | <p>- Hold a certain number of platform tokens. </p><p>- Extended reward-earning potential.</p>                                                                               | <p>- Hold a certain number of platform tokens.</p><p>- Enhanced earning rate per view. <br>- Ability to upload up to 5 videos per week. <br>- Access to enhanced analytics and collaboration opportunities.</p>                         |
|                                                                                                         |                                                                                                                                                                              |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Luminary Tier** (Viewers) / **Premium Tier** (Creators)                                               | <p>- Purchase and hold an exclusive platform NFT.  </p><p>- Unlimited earning potential with no daily cap. </p><p>- Access to premium content and features.</p>              | <p>- Purchase and hold a membership NFT. </p><p>- Highest earning rate per view. </p><p>- Unlimited video uploads. </p><p>- Access to premium content placement and advanced analytics. </p><p>- Direct support from platform team.</p> |
| **Guardian Tier**                                                                                       | <p>- Combination of holding platform tokens and an exclusive NFT. </p><p>- All benefits of previous tiers plus ad-free experience.</p>                                       | <p>- Combination of holding platform tokens and a membership NFT. </p><p>- All benefits of previous tiers plus priority content featuring and community leader badge.</p>                                                               |

This table serves as a guide for both viewers and creators to understand what is required to access each tier and the benefits associated with them. The criteria are designed to encourage both engagement with the platform's content and investment in its token and NFT ecosystem.

_**Note:** The access criteria and benefits associated with each tier are subject to change as we approach the official launch of the platform. We are committed to continually refining and optimizing our tier system to best serve the needs and interests of our community._
